About Us

Welcome to the future of marketing with EVA Ad Ventures, a pioneering subsidiary of EVA Enterprises. At EVA Ad Ventures, we merge cutting-edge technology with marketing prowess to redefine the advertising landscape. Our mission is simple: democratize advertising, making it accessible and impactful for all. Founded by esteemed IIT Alumni and supported by a team of skilled IIT Alumni, EVA Ad Ventures embodies expertise, innovation, and a commitment to transforming the Indian marketing landscape.

Our Mission and Vision

At EVA Ad Ventures, our mission is to revolutionize the advertising industry by seamlessly integrating technology with marketing strategies. We aim to introduce state-of-the-art digital screens capable of showcasing immersive 3D animations and holographic displays in key locations across India.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our individuality, our diversity, and our sheer determination. We are all aligned towards a common goal of creating a national platform for marketing and offering high-end marketing and advertising experience to all, including small and big businesses at affordable prices.
